About Us

Welcome to Jimmy’s Craft Creations. Jim’s Crafty Creations (PTY) LTD is a dynamic and diversified service company built on a strong foundation of experience and growth. The company is a continuation of JimCatech Investment, which was established in 2016.

We specialize in the installation and maintenance of CCTV surveillance systems, electric fencing, intercom systems, access control, intruder alarms, and motorized gates — all designed to enhance safety and strengthen property protection.
